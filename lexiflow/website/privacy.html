Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal data

Last updated: June 18, 2025

At LexiFlow, we take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • User Account: Email, name (optional), encrypted password
  • Waitlist: Email address only
  • Payment: Processed by Stripe (we do NOT store your payment information)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Extension: Translation history (stored locally on your device)
  • Flashcards: Card content (local for free, cloud for Premium)
  • Usage: Number of translations, languages used (anonymized)
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: We NEVER collect the content of web pages you visit, only the words you choose to translate.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your LexiFlow account
  • Provide translation and flashcard services
  • Synchronize your data (Premium only)
  • Send important emails (account, payment)
  • Improve our services (anonymized data)

2.1 Marketing

We will ONLY send marketing emails if you have explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time.

3. Data Sharing

We NEVER sell your personal data.

We share your data only with:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Translation services: Text to translate only (no personal info)
  • Legal authorities: If required by law

4. Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is

  • Free Plan: Everything is stored locally in your browser
  • Premium Plan: Data synchronized on our secure servers (EU)
  • Payments: Handled by Stripe (PCI compliant)

4.2 Security

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all communications
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Limited and monitored server access
  • Regular encrypted backups

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify incorrect information
  • Delete your account and data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data (portability)
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights:

Email us at: privacy@lexiflow.com
We will respond within 30 days maximum.

6. Cookies

LexiFlow uses essential cookies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your language preferences
  • Ensure account security

We do NOT use tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Children

LexiFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Important changes will be notified by email.

9. Contact

For any questions about your data:

Email: privacy@lexiflow.com
Address: [To be completed with your address]

In summary: We collect the minimum necessary data, protect it carefully, and never sell it. Your data belongs to you.
