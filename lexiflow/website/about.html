The Story Behind LexiFlow

It all started with a moment of frustration. You know, that moment when you're reading a fascinating article and... BAM! An unknown word. You copy it, open a new tab, paste it into Google Translate, go back to your article... and you've lost the flow. Frustrating, right?

But the worst part? 5 minutes later, you've already forgotten that word. All that time wasted for nothing. That's when we thought: there must be a better way!

LexiFlow was born from this double frustration: why do we have to interrupt our reading to go translate a word elsewhere? And why translate if we're going to forget it in a few minutes?

That's why LexiFlow combines instant translation (without leaving your page) and smart flashcards to transform each translation into lasting learning. Translate, memorize, master - all naturally while browsing.
